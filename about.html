The College of Business's computer labs provide faculty, staff, and students with essential technologies, including computer workstations, laptops, and computer accessories. Along with the Joseph C. & Carrie J. Hete Technology Common there are three teaching labs which give students easy access to software, networking, and printing. Laptops are also available for loan on an as-is and as-available basis.
